Who I Am

I am Arik Mukherjee, a passionate Computer Science student and Data Science enthusiast from West Bengal, India. Currently pursuing my Master of Science in Computer Science at West Bengal State University (2025–27), I bring a strong academic foundation with a stellar 9.56 CGPA in my Bachelor's degree from Dum Dum Motijheel College.

"I believe that the intersection of data and human insight can solve the most complex problems of our time — and that's exactly the space I want to operate in."

What I Do

My work spans the full spectrum of data science and software development. I design and build predictive machine learning models, develop full-stack web applications, and explore Generative AI systems. Through internships at Anwesha Academy and IDEAS Technology Innovation Hub at ISI Kolkata, I have gained hands-on experience applying data science to real-world problems.

Beyond data, I am a developer with experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, Python, C, and C++. I have built projects ranging from health prediction systems to full-stack e-learning platforms.

Where I'm Headed

My goal is to build a career at the forefront of Data Science, Machine Learning, and AI research. I aim to contribute to projects that have meaningful social impact — from healthcare analytics to intelligent education systems. I am particularly interested in Generative AI and its applications in real-world domains.
